## Glossary of Statistical Terms

**English–Lithuanian–French–Russian–German**, edited by Antanas Buračas (Mykolas Romeris University, 2007, 878 p.; ISBN 978-9955-19-084-4).

This edition is devoted to the standardization of Lithuanian statistical terms adequate to those used in English, French, Russian and German. It comprises **eight dictionaries**: a multilingual table of statistical terms together with seven bilingual dictionaries (each paired with Lithuanian).

The multilingual glossary is based largely on the terminological web edition of *The International Statistical Institute* and *The Oxford Dictionary of Statistical Terms* (2003), edited by M. G. Kendall, F. H. C. Marriott and W. R. Buckland (6th ed. by Yadolah Dodge), with many additional corrections for multilingual semantics.
