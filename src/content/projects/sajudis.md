## Sąjūdis

The **Lithuanian National Independence Movement "Sąjūdis"** led the country's peaceful drive to restore independence. From 1988 to 1992 Antanas Buračas was a founding member of the movement, a member of its First and Second Seimas and their Councils, and a signatory of the Gotland Memorandum.

## The Gotland Memorandum

The **Gotland Memorandum** expressed the Lithuanian unity, around the world, for the reestablishment of state independence. It was issued in several languages — Lithuanian, English, German and French — copies of which are preserved in the photoarchive below.
