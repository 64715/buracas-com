## Adaptation of International Classifications in Lithuania

**English–Lithuanian–English.** Consulting in classification and finance terminology by Prof. Antanas Buračas, Chairman of the Commission for International Classificators, with Dr. Jonas Markelevičius.

Systems adapted include **EUROVOC, SNA, ISIC, CPC, CPA, COICOP, COFOG, NACE, PRODCOM, MGFS, ISCO** and others. Published editions (Government of the Republic of Lithuania, Vilnius, 1999) include:

- **BOPM** — Balance of Payments Manual: Classification and Standard Components, 75 p.
- **CPC** — Provisional Central Product Classification (Eurostat), 247 p.
- **COFOG, COICOP, COPNI** — Functional Classifications of the 1993 SNA, 106 p.
- **MGFS** — A Manual on Government Finance Statistics: Classification, 122 p.
- **NACE rev. 1** — Statistical Classification of Economic Activities in the European Community (Eurostat), 193 p.
- **SNA** — System of National Accounts 1993: Classifications and Accounts (IMF), 112 p.
- **ISIC rev. 3** — International Standard Industrial Classification of All Economic Activities (UN), 171 p.

The original classificators (in English) are published by Eurostat. Inquiries and proposals for joint editing or distribution are welcome.
