## About

*Old Types of the Grand Duchy of Lithuania* (Vilnius, 2004; ISBN 5-420-01540-4) presents a study and visual record drawing on historical iconography of the peoples and figures of the Grand Duchy of Lithuania, continuing the author's editorial work on Lithuanian cultural heritage.
